    Represents the specific error type returned by the miiCard API.

    package MiiCardConsumers

     Constants

     

    Signifies that the miiCard member has revoked access to your app.

    ACCESS_REVOKED 

    Your application needs to make the member go through the OAuth process again to obtain fresh access tokens if you wish to use the API on their behalf.

     

    Signifies that your application has been suspended.

    BLACKLISTED 

    No API access can take place until miiCard releases the suspension on your application.

     

    Signifies that your account's transactional status is development-only.

    DEVELOPMENT_TRANSACTIONAL_SUPPORT_ONLY 

    This is the case when your application hasn't yet been made live in the miiCard system, for example while we process your billing details and perform final checks.

     

    Signifies that a more general exception took place during the call.

    EXCEPTION 

    Further information may be available by calling the MiiApiResponse's getErrorMessage() function.

     

    Signifies that the snapshot ID was either invalid.

    INVALID_SNAPSHOT_ID 

    The ID may not exist, or doesn't belong to the user whose access tokens were used to make the API request.

     

    Signifies that your application has been deleted.

    PRODUCT_DELETED 

    miiCard members will be unable to go through the OAuth process for your application, nor will you be able to access identity details through the API.

     

    Signifies that your application has been disabled.

    PRODUCT_DISABLED 

    Your applications can be disabled either by your request or by miiCard. miiCard members will be unable to go through the OAuth process for your application, though you will still be able to access the API.

     

    Signifies that there was no error.
